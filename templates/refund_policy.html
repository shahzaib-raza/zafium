Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At Zafium, we are committed to delivering high-quality digital services and ensuring customer satisfaction. Because our services are custom and often begin immediately after an order is placed, refunds are handled according to the policy below.

1. Custom Digital Services

Zafium provides customized digital services including web development, graphic design, AI solutions, automation, research, data processing, video production, and related professional services.

Each project is created specifically for the client and cannot generally be returned like a physical product.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests are considered on a case-by-case basis. A refund may be granted if:

  • No work has been started on the project.
  • The order was placed accidentally and work has not begun.
  • We determine that we are unable to provide the requested service.
  • A duplicate payment was made.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will generally not be available if:

  • Work has already commenced.
  • Project deliverables have been completed or substantially completed.
  • The customer changes their mind after work has started.
  • The customer fails to provide required information, files, or feedback.
  • The project is delayed due to the customer's actions or inactivity.

4. Project Revisions

Before considering a refund, we encourage customers to request revisions where appropriate. Reasonable revisions that remain within the original project scope may be provided to resolve issues and ensure satisfaction.

5. Duplicate or Incorrect Payments

If you are charged more than once for the same order or believe a payment was processed incorrectly, please contact us promptly. Verified duplicate or incorrect payments will be refunded.

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are issued through our payment provider, using the original payment method.

Once approved, processing times depend on your payment provider or financial institution and may take several business days.

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us with:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your order number
  • The reason for your refund request

We will review your request and respond as soon as reasonably possible.

8. Chargebacks

If you experience an issue with your order, we encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your payment provider. Most issues can be resolved quickly through direct communication.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with the revised effective date.
