Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Zafium ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide when using our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Ordered services
  • Project requirements and uploaded information

Payment card information is never collected or stored by Zafium. All payment processing is securely handled by our gateway provider.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage your orders.
  • Deliver purchased digital services.
  • Communicate project updates.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve our services and website.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are securely processed by Our gateway provider, Merchant of Record. Our gateway provider manages payment processing, taxation, invoicing, fraud prevention, and payment security.

Your payment information is submitted directly to our gateway provider and is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve website performance, remember user preferences, and analyze website usage. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Protection

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your information may only be shared with trusted providers when necessary, including:

  • Gateway provider (payment processing)
  • Email delivery providers
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Legal authorities where required by law

7. Data Retention

We retain customer information only for as long as necessary to deliver services, maintain project records, comply with legal requirements, and resolve disputes.

8. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your information where legally permitted.
  • Withdraw communication preferences.

9. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page with the revised effective date.
